SUMMARY OF FINDINGS
================================================================================

✅ STRENGTHS CONFIRMED:
1. Dual format support works correctly (energy vs target_energy, genre vs favorite_genre)
2. NULL preference handling works (doesn't default to 0.5, skips missing criteria)
3. Boundary value handling is robust (0.5 divisor, 0.6 threshold both work)
4. Edge cases handled gracefully (extreme values, missing moods, contradictions)
5. PERFECT MATCH DETECTION works (Zero Energy Gap scores 4.50/4.50)

⚠️ BEHAVIOR TO UNDERSTAND:
1. Contradictory preferences (high energy + sad mood): Scorer ranks independently
   - Both criteria are evaluated; neither overrides the other
   - If mood has no matches, energy determines ranking
   
2. Impossible combinations (metal + acoustic): Best match in genre still wins
   - Genre match (+2.0) often dominates other factors
   - Impossible preferences don't prevent scoring

3. Weighting imbalance potential:
   - Genre (2.0) + Mood (1.0) = 3.0 max from categorical
   - Energy (1.0) + Acousticness (0.5) = 1.5 max from numeric
   - Categorical preferences strongly influence ranking

RECOMMENDATIONS FOR FUTURE REFINEMENT:
- Consider if genre/mood should have equal weight to energy/acousticness
- Document expected behavior for contradictory preferences
- Test with larger song dataset to find edge cases with more matches
- Consider adding more numeric preference factors (valence, danceability, tempo)
